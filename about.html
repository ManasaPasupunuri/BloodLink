Our Mission

BloodLink Rescue Network connects blood donors with patients in need, ensuring that life-saving blood is available when and where it's needed most.

Founded in 2020, we have grown from a small initiative to a nationwide network with thousands of registered donors and over 200 partner hospitals.

Our Story

BloodLink was born from a crisis that became a calling.

When our founder, Dr. Sarah Chen, witnessed a hospital struggling to find blood donors during an emergency, she recognized the need for a better system. What started as a simple spreadsheet and phone calls evolved into a nationwide platform powered by technology and community support.

Today, BloodLink stands as a bridge between those who give blood and those who need it—united by compassion and the shared goal of saving lives.
